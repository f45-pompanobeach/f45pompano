Frequently Asked Questions

Before You Buy
Q

Do I need any experience?

A

Absolutely not! F45 is designed for all fitness levels, whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete. You can modify your exercises and go at your own pace. If it's your first class, just let the coach know — they'll take care of you.

Q

Who qualifies for this offer?

A

This offer is for first-time local residents only. Qualifying zip codes: 33060, 33062, 33064, 33069, 33308, 33309, 33334, 33441. We'll verify your ID at your first class. If your ID doesn't show a qualifying zip, email us a lease or recent electric bill with your name and address before your first class.

Q

Does this offer roll into a membership?

A

No — this is a standalone trial offer with no strings attached. After your first class, we'd love to chat about membership options. We want you to experience F45 first and fall in love with it before we ever talk numbers. No pressure.

Q

Can I bring a friend?

A

Absolutely — working out with a friend makes it even better! If your friend is also a first-time local resident (same zip code criteria) and 18 or older, they're welcome to join on the same offer.

Before Your First Class
Q

What should I wear and bring?

A

Wear comfortable workout clothes and supportive sneakers. For your first class, arrive 15 minutes early and bring your picture ID. For all classes bring a water bottle and a sweat towel (mandatory). Don't have a towel? You can purchase one from us.

Q

Where are you located?

A

We are located a few blocks north of Atlantic Blvd on Federal Hwy/US-1. We're in the same plaza as West Marine and Zona Fresca, across from Pompano Beach park — 824 N Federal Hwy, Pompano Beach, FL 33062.

Q

Where do I park?

A

We have parking available in the plaza — no need to stress about finding a spot. Just pull in and come straight to us at 824 N Federal Hwy.

Q

Is there a shower?

A

We don't have shower facilities, so plan accordingly. Most members head home or to work right after class — bring a change of clothes if needed and don't forget your sweat towel!

During Your Trial
Q

What if I can't use all 3 classes in 7 days?

A

Since this is a special trial offer, the 3 classes must be used within 7 days of your first session — no extensions. Most members tell us they knew F45 was for them after just 1 or 2 classes!

Q

What is an InBody scan?

A

An InBody scan measures your body composition — muscle mass, body fat, and body water. It's included with your trial! For your 2nd or 3rd class, arrive 10–15 minutes early and ask your coach to set it up for you.
